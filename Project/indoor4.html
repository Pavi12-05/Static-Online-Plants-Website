Coleus painted nettle

Rs.120

Coleus scutellarioides is a perennial plant with multicolor foliage. Ovate leaves are mostly covered by chocolate brown & center leaf portion spreads paint like pink in color. Coleus scutellarioides foliage gives a contrasting appearance in the garden area. Inflorescence (flowers) present at the end of the stem with few or many flowers. Coleus painted nettle plant gives a good ornament look.
